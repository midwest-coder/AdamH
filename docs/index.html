About

Adam Joel believes that the most important role he will ever take on is that of himself. He strives daily to improve his own character, growing and expanding his life experience, so that he may better be of service to the roles he will embody.

Actors have the ability to move audiences deeply, ask tough questions, effect change, and free people from concern. Adam’s greatest ambition is to be of service to the world through his acting and storytelling by revealing his raw humanity, discovering himself anew in each project, with each new tribe.

After struggling mightily for many years, Adam became clean and sober in January 2020 and attributes this breakthrough to every achievement he has had since. He’s extremely grateful for another chance at life and for all the support he has received. His mission is to give himself over entirely in order to be of optimal service as a man first and then as an actor.

After all, as Shakespeare said, “All the world’s a stage, And all the men and women merely players; They have their exits and their entrances; And one man in his time plays many parts…”
